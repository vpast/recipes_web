BBQ Tofu Sandwich

Minutes to cook - 55

Ingredients:

  • Bread: 10 slice, 280g
  • Tofu: 1, 324g
  • Barbecue Sauce: cup 1 1/2, 375g
  • Tomatoes: 1, 123g
  • Lettuce: 10 leaf, 240g

Nutrion:

  • Calories: 1879g
  • Carbs: 298g
  • Fat: 41g
  • Protein: 93g

Cooking Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Prepare tofu by pressing out the water (one good strategy is to freeze in package, thaw in fridge, and then press. This will give it a better texture).
  3. Cover tofu with BBQ Sauce and place on lined baking sheet.
  4. Place in oven for 45 minutes, check regularly and add brush on more sauce as it dries.
  5. Remove from oven and assemble sandwich.
  6. Best served on toasted sourdough, but personal preference will dictate bread type.
