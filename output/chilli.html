30 Minutes Chilli

Minutes to cook - 20

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef: 1 piece, 453g
  • Onions: 3/4 cup, 120g
  • Tomatoes: 1 3/4 cup, 420g
  • Kidney Beans: 1 3/4 cup, 448g
  • Diced Tomatoes With Green Chillies: 10 oz, 284g
  • Seasoning mix dry chilli Spices: 5 tbsp, 33.8g
  • Tomato sauce: 1 cup, 245g
  • Water: 1 cup, 237g
  • Chedar Cheese: 1 cup of shredded cheese, 113g

Nutrion:

  • Calories: 2320g
  • Carbs: 134g
  • Fat: 136g
  • Protein: 142g

Cooking Steps:

  1. Cook beef and onions in large saucepan over medium-high heat 5-10 minutes or until beef is crumbled, cooked through, and no longer pink. Stir occasionally; drain.
  2. Stir in seasoning mix. Add remaining ingredients (excluding cheese); bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium; simmer 10 minutes, stirring several times.
  3. Serve hot topped with the cheese and enjoy!
